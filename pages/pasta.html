Pasta Recipe

Description: This pasta uses fresh sliced tomatoes and a heavy cream based sauce to make a classic tasting pasta recipe

Ingredients:

  • Box of Pasta
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • garlic
  • spinach
  • olive oil
  • parmesan cheese
  • heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. First, boil the pasta until it is al dente
  2. Next, put a large pan on medium-high heat and start cooking the garlic in olive oil
  3. Once the garlic is golden brown, add the spinach and tomatoes to the pan
  4. once the spinach starts to wilt add the heavy cream and parmesan cheese, then lower the pan's heat to a simmer
  5. Finally add the boiled pasta to the sauce and mix it thoroughly
  6. Enjoy!
