Glass of Water

Description: Water, you need atleast 8 glasses a day so let me teach you how to make it

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Optional: Ice

Instructions:

  1. Take glass and put it under sink
  2. Turn on the sink
  3. be careful not to put it on the hot side
  4. Optional: add ice to the water to enjoy it cold
  5. Enjoy!
