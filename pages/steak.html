Steak Recipe

Description: This is my signature steak recipe. It is quite simple but very flavorful

Ingredients:

  • sirloin steak
  • butter
  • salt and pepper
  • fresh rosemary
  • garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. First coat the pan in butter, pam or olive oil and set to medium-high heat
  2. Season both sides of the steak with a generous amount of salt pepper and garlic powder
  3. Add the steak to the pan once it is hot
  4. Place the fresh rosemary ontop of the steak
  5. Give the steak roughly 5 minutes on each side
  6. When you flip the steak add more butter ontop of it to soak the steak while it is still cooking
  7. Use a spoon and safely scoop the steak juice from the pan back on the steak
  8. Once steak is medium-rare (as it should be) take it off the pan and let it rest for 5 minutes
  9. Add leftover juice in the pan to the steak while it is resting
  10. Once rested, Enjoy!
